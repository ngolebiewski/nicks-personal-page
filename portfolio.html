Prints for Ukraine

Technology: Old-fashioned watercolor paint, Etsy

At the start of the Ukraine-Russia war in 2022, I wondered what I could do to help as an artist. My idea, do what I do well, make a painting. I made a watercolor painting of the Ukrainian restaurant Veselka*, in the East Village. The owner purchased the original and we both promoted a sale of prints of the painting on our Instagram pages. All proceeds were donated to fund hospitals in the Ukraine, all $1,000 of it.
